Who We Are

Solutions Energy Services was established in the year 2015, in Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia as a 100% Saudi Owned Company.

A leader in the truest sense of the word. It has guided the company through over 6 years of successful business experience in Saudi Arabia. Our management inspires and integrates enthusiasm, dynamism, and industry expertise to provide the highest value proposition services and customized solutions for innovative and continuous operation throughout the year regardless of the cycle of weathers.

We route our evolution through striving to maintain and service our existing projects well, enabling us to establish our reputation as innovative service providers to win over newer projects in the Industry.

In maintaining international standards, to assure our Service Quality and to maintain a healthy, ethical, and efficient working ambiance, we have developed, maintained, and implemented our Management System by benchmarking our Key Performance Indicator with International Standards, Regulations, and Work practices. Hence, we proudly ensure that our standards exceed the requirements of ISO.

9001, ISO 45001 and ISO 14001. Our quality control department ensures that our services comply effectively, creating more satisfied clientele within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Our multi-national & multi-discipline work force including Managers, Engineers, Professionals, Supervisors, Foremen and various technicians work as a team to execute awarded projects with ease by having at hand a precise equipment inventory, which entails resources that range from smaller tools to heavy construction equipment. We have the right combination of workforce resources that can take up any challenge & execute every task to completion with no exception.

It is with grand vision that our CEO, Mr. Abdul Monem H. AlJumah has extended a warm welcome to PMPs (Project Management Professionals) to become an integral part of the company with tangible ‘GOAL-SETTING’ to set new records of promising Specialized Industrial Services. Solutions Energy Services always aims to ensure an efficient service with the right technology and the correct techniques, enabling the company to maintain a trust-based relationship with its clients, providing truly cost-effective solutions and remain “YOUR PARTNER OF CHOICE”

HSSE POLICY

At Directional Goals, we care for our people and the environment. We ensure that our people have a safe, healthy, and secure workplace; this is a fundamental right. This policy explains how we provide this.

We will:

  • Care for our people.
  • Identify and manage hazards to eliminate or mitigate resultant risks.
  • Prevent injury, ill-health, pollution, and loss resulting from our activities.
  • Be responsible in our approach to protecting the environment and minimizing our impacts.
  • Deliver continual improvement in our health, safety, security, and environmental performance.

We do this by:

  • Ensuring we have exemplary HSSE leadership and management.
  • Having effective, efficient, and applied HSSE management systems.
  • Understanding and complying with all legal, industry, and other external requirements.
  • Establishing and attaining clear HSSE objectives.
  • Learning lessons from our incidents and preventing reoccurrence.
  • Engaging with our people on HSSE issues.
  • Working with our customers, regulators, and others to promote continuous improvement.
  • Training our people to be competent and safe in undertaking their roles.
  • Helping our supply chain and partners to meet our own policy obligations.
  • Promoting a positive HSSE culture that drives HSSE improvement.
  • Encouraging anyone to stop a job if they perceive any HSSE shortfall.
